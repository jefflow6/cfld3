Chest X-ray

A chest x-ray produces images of the heart, lungs, airways, blood vessels and the bones of the spine and chest.

Pulmonary Function Testing

A test that measures lung volume, capacity, rates of flow, and gas exchange.

Spirometry

A test that measures how much air you inhale, how much you exhale and how quickly you exhale.

6 Minute Walk Study

Is used to judge exercise tolerance, abnormal breathing patterns and possibly the need for supplemental oxygen.

Vaccinations

We offer Seasonal Flu, Pneumovax23, Prevnar13, and Xolair injections.

Home Sleep Testing

A take home sleep test to diagnose Sleep Apnea or other sleep disorders.

Electrocardiogram

A test that measures your heart's electrical activity and can measure how fast the heart beats to how well its chambers conduct electrical energy.

CPAP / BiPAP Services

CPAP/BIPAP monitoring for compliance as well as offering masks and tubing.
