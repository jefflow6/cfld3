Consultants for Lung Diseases Medical Group

Wayneinder Anand M.D.

Is board-certifed in Adult/Pediatric Sleep Medicine, Pulmonary Medicine, Internal Medicine, and Critical Care.

He earned his medical degree from American University of the Caribbean School of Medicine in St. Maarten. He completed his internship and residency at MetroHealth Medical Center in Cleveland.

Dr. Anand was voted a Top 10 Doctor in the Los Angeles metropolitan area in 2014. He was also honored with the Patient's Choice Award in 2011, and the Compassionate Doctor Recognition in 2010.

He is affiliated with Providence St. Joseph Medical Center.

Dr. Anand's clinical areas of expertise also include specialized training in sleep medicine as well as pulmonary medicine.

Dr. Anand exclusively see patients at our Burbank office Monday-Friday. Call to schedule an appointment at 818-842-4819

Specializes in treatment of:

  • Obstructive Sleep Apnea OSA
  • Insomnia
  • Narcolepsy
  • COPD
  • Cough
  • Bronchitis
  • Pneumonia
  • Allergies
  • Emphysema
