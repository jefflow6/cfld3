Consultants for Lung Diseases Medical Group

Steven Taback M.D. FACP, FCCP

A board-certified pulmonologist, critical care spectialst, and internist, with over 27 years of experience in medcial practice.

Dr Taback has a bachelor of arts degree from Occidental College with a degree in psychology/biology. He received his medical doctorate from the Chicago Medical School at Rosalind Franklin University. He completed his fellowship and residency at LAC USC Medical Center in Los Angeles.

He is a diplomate of the American Board of Internal Medicine and a diplomate of the National Board of Medical Examiners. Dr Taback has also been elected a fellow of the American College of Chest Physicians.

Dr Taback is affiliated with Providence St Joseph Medcial Center in Burbank and USC Verdugo Hills Hospital. He sees patients in both our Burbank and Glendale offices.
